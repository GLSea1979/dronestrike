    WHAT

  1. This is an app designed to visually represent data collected about drone strikes carried out by the U.S. military.


    2. WHERE

  2. The data we have show strikes that have occured in three countries in the middle east; Pakistan, Yemen and Somalia.


    3. HOW

  3. The map is responsive and you are able to click on a country and see more in depth of the specific strikes. The markers show each US drone strikes with details of dates, fatalities using an API from dronestre.am.com.


    4. WHO

  4. It is not possible to tell exactly who is in the strike zone outside of the targeted individuals. Unfortunately, many young children and innocent people are victims of these attacks.
